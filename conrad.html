Conrad Kramer


@conradev

Conrad is the co-founder at DeskConnect, the company behind WorkFlow app that won WWDC design award in 2015. He started developing for iOS after he got involved with the jailbreak scene back in 2010 with his first tweak Graviboard. Since then, he has gotten into regular iOS development and worked on various open source projects in the Cocoa community, like AFOAuth2Client and WFNotificationCenter.

He now spends all of his waking hours on Workflow where he works on anything from the server backend to building the complex drag and drop interactions.
